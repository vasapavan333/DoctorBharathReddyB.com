Services & Specializations

Comprehensive Orthopedic Care for All Your Bone & Joint Needs

Joint Replacement Surgery

Knee & Hip Replacement

Advanced joint replacement procedures using the latest techniques and materials. We specialize in total knee replacement (TKR), total hip replacement (THR), and partial joint replacements with minimal invasive approaches for faster recovery.

  • Total Knee Replacement (TKR)
  • Total Hip Replacement (THR)
  • Partial Knee Replacement
  • Revision Joint Replacement
  • Minimally Invasive Techniques
  • Computer-Assisted Surgery

Fracture Treatment

All Types of Bone Fractures

Expert diagnosis and treatment of fractures ranging from simple to complex. We use modern fixation techniques including plates, screws, intramedullary nails, and external fixators to ensure proper healing and restoration of function.

  • Open & Closed Fracture Management
  • Internal Fixation (Plates & Screws)
  • External Fixation
  • Intramedullary Nailing
  • Non-Surgical Fracture Care
  • Bone Grafting Procedures

Sports Injuries

Athlete Recovery & Rehabilitation

Specialized care for athletes and active individuals. We understand the urgency of sports injuries and provide prompt diagnosis, treatment, and rehabilitation to get you back to your sport as quickly and safely as possible.

  • ACL/PCL Reconstruction
  • Meniscus Repair & Surgery
  • Shoulder & Rotator Cuff Injuries
  • Ankle & Foot Injuries
  • Arthroscopic Surgery
  • Sports-Specific Rehabilitation

Spine Care

Back Pain & Spinal Conditions

Comprehensive treatment for spinal disorders including disc problems, spinal deformities, and back pain. We offer both conservative and surgical approaches tailored to each patient's condition and needs.

  • Disc Herniation Treatment
  • Spinal Fusion Surgery
  • Scoliosis Correction
  • Spinal Decompression
  • Minimally Invasive Spine Surgery
  • Back Pain Management

Arthritis Treatment

Osteoarthritis & Rheumatoid Arthritis

Comprehensive management of arthritis to reduce pain, improve function, and enhance quality of life. Treatment plans include medications, injections, physical therapy, and surgical options when necessary.

  • Osteoarthritis Management
  • Rheumatoid Arthritis Care
  • Joint Injections (Hyaluronic Acid, Steroids)
  • Medication Management
  • Physical Therapy Programs
  • Surgical Interventions

Pediatric Orthopedics

Children's Bone & Joint Care

Specialized orthopedic care for children and adolescents. We understand the unique needs of growing bones and provide gentle, age-appropriate treatment for congenital conditions, injuries, and developmental issues.

  • Congenital Deformities
  • Growth Plate Injuries
  • Pediatric Fractures
  • Limb Deformity Correction
  • Clubfoot Treatment
  • Developmental Hip Dysplasia

Trauma & Emergency Care

24/7 Emergency Orthopedic Services

Round-the-clock emergency orthopedic care for acute injuries, accidents, and traumatic conditions. Our team is equipped to handle complex trauma cases requiring immediate surgical intervention.

  • Acute Fracture Management
  • Polytrauma Care
  • Emergency Surgery
  • Accident & Injury Treatment
  • 24/7 Emergency Availability
  • Critical Care Coordination
